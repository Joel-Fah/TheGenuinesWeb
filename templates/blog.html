What can The Genuines equip you with?

Computer Science Paper 1 & 2 Advanced Level
Theoretical Sessions

This book is a special sequel to the G.C.E Advanced Level Computer Science. The book has been written with special attention to all candidates willing to understand the concepts in Advanced Level, with the belief being “a constant solving of past examination papers drives the concept faster”.
This book is divided into three main parts; the first part consists of past GCE paper 1 questions from 2010 to 2019, the second part consist of past GCE paper 2 questions from 2010 to 2019 and four additional regional mock exams and the third part is composed of solutions to the preceding past GCE questions which are clearly fleshed out demonstrated.
The aim of this workbook is not for you to memorize solutions but to actually understand the concept behind each problem. That is why I emphasized on the terms “clearly fleshed out demonstrated solutions”.

Computer Science Paper 3 Advanced Level
Practical Sessions

This book is divided into three main parts; the first part consists of past GCE paper 3 questions from 2010 to 2020 together with sample and regional mock questions, the second part consists of proposed solutions to the preceding past GCE and past Mock questions which are clearly fleshed out demonstrated and the third part is composed of short, simple and summarized notes to accompany students during their study times.
It is hoped that a meticulous examination of these questions (after treatment of each year session) would help candidates to secure the grades they desire and deserve. Candidates preparing for competitive entrance examinations that require knowledge of Computer Science shall also find this workbook very useful.
